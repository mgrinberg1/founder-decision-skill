# Your Project Context (template)

Copy this file to `your-context.md` in the same folder and fill it in. The decision
copilot reads `your-context.md` automatically (if it exists) so its analysis is anchored
to *your* venture instead of generic advice. Delete the prompts you don't want to answer —
partial context still helps. Keep it short; this is a briefing, not a business plan.

## What we do
One or two lines: the product and the problem it solves.

## Who it's for
The customer / ICP. B2B or B2C? Who actually pays?

## Stage
Idea / pre-launch / early revenue / scaling. Roughly how much traction (users, revenue)?

## Business model
How you make money (subscription, one-time, usage, etc.) and rough unit economics if known.

## Market & competition
The market you're in, key competitors, and what (if anything) makes you different.

## Constraints that shape decisions
Runway / cash position, team size, geography and its quirks (regulation, payments, sales
cycle length), founder situation (full-time / side project), anything that meaningfully
narrows your options.

## Current top priorities / open questions
What you're most focused on right now, and the big uncertainties you're wrestling with.
